About LUCAS Token

🎓 Student Innovation

LUCAS Token is an educational cryptocurrency project created by a computer science student at the Federal University of Pará (UFPA), Brazil. This project represents the intersection of academic learning and real-world blockchain technology.

🔗 Built on Polygon

Deployed on the Polygon network for fast, low-cost transactions. LUCAS leverages the efficiency of Layer 2 scaling to provide an accessible entry point into the cryptocurrency ecosystem.

🌟 Community-Driven

Join a growing community of supporters who believe in student-led innovation and the democratization of blockchain technology. Every holder is part of our educational journey.
